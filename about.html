One stop shop for all your transport needs

3N TO SACCO is a Savings and Credit Cooperative based in Nakuru County that operates public service transportaion. Our matatus ply the Lanet-Ndundori road and ferry commuters as well as their goods. Commuters can now reserve tickets online through our integrated online ticketing system.
As always commuter satisfaction is at the core of our business.
